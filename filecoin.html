Filecoin Chart

Filecoin is a decentralized storage network that allows users to rent out unused storage space to other users. The platform is designed to provide a more efficient and cost-effective way of storing and accessing data compared to traditional cloud storage services. Filecoin has gained widespread popularity and is now being used by a variety of businesses and organizations around the world.

For more information about Filecoin, visit the official website at https://filecoin.io/.
