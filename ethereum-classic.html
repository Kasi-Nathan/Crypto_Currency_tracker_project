Ethereum Classic Chart

Ethereum Classic (ETC) is a decentralized platform that runs smart contracts, similar to Ethereum. ETC is the original Ethereum blockchain that did not undergo a hard fork after the DAO hack in 2016, which resulted in the creation of Ethereum (ETH) and Ethereum Classic (ETC). The ETC community believes in the principle of "Code is Law" and is committed to maintaining the integrity of the blockchain.

For more information about Ethereum Classic, visit the official website at https://ethereumclassic.org/.
