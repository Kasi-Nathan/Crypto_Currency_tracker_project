Uniswap Chart

Uniswap is a decentralized cryptocurrency exchange that allows users to trade ERC-20 tokens without the need for an intermediary or central authority. Uniswap is built on the Ethereum blockchain and uses smart contracts to facilitate the exchange of tokens. The platform is known for its automated market maker (AMM) system, which allows users to trade tokens in a liquidity pool.

For more information about Uniswap, visit the official website at https://uniswap.org/.
