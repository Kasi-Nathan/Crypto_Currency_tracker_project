Monero Chart

Monero (XMR) is a privacy-focused cryptocurrency that was launched in 2014. It is designed to be secure, private, and untraceable, and uses advanced cryptography to protect the identities of its users. Monero has gained a reputation as the leading privacy coin and is widely used by individuals and businesses that value their privacy.

For more information about Monero, visit the official website at https://www.getmonero.org/.
