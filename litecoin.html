Litecoin Chart

Litecoin is a peer-to-peer cryptocurrency created by Charlie Lee in 2011 as an alternative to Bitcoin. It is based on the Bitcoin protocol but with significant differences, such as a shorter block generation time and a different hashing algorithm. Litecoin has gained widespread acceptance and is now traded on numerous cryptocurrency exchanges.

For more information about Litecoin, visit the official website at https://litecoin.org/.
