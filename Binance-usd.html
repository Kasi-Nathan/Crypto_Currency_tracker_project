Binance USD Chart

Binance USD (BUSD) is a stablecoin pegged to the value of the US dollar. It is issued by Binance, one of the largest cryptocurrency exchanges in the world. BUSD is designed to provide traders and investors with a stable and reliable digital asset that can be used for trading, remittances, and other purposes.

For more information about Binance USD, visit the official website at https://www.binance.com/en/busd.
