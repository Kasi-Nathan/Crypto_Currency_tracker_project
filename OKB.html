OKB Chart

OKB is the native token of OKEx, one of the world's leading cryptocurrency exchanges. OKB can be used to pay for trading fees on OKEx, as well as to participate in token sales on OKEx Jumpstart and to receive discounts on other services on the platform. OKB has a total supply of 300 million tokens, with 60% of the supply allocated for trading rewards, 10% for marketing and community building, and 30% for platform development.

For more information about OKB, visit the official website at https://www.okex.com/okb.
