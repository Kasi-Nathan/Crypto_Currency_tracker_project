Cardano Chart

Cardano is a decentralized platform that is designed to allow developers to create smart contracts and decentralized applications. It was created by IOHK, a blockchain research and development company, and launched in 2017. Cardano uses a proof-of-stake consensus mechanism, which is more energy-efficient than the proof-of-work mechanism used by Bitcoin.

For more information about Cardano, visit the official website at https://cardano.org/.
