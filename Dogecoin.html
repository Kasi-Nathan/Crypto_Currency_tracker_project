Dogecoin Chart

Dogecoin is a decentralized, peer-to-peer digital currency that was created in 2013. It is based on the popular "Doge" internet meme and was initially developed as a lighthearted alternative to Bitcoin. Dogecoin has since gained a strong following and has become a popular cryptocurrency for tipping and microtransactions.

For more information about Dogecoin, visit the official website at https://dogecoin.com/.
