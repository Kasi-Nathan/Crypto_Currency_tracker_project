LEO Token Chart

The LEO Token is a utility token issued by Bitfinex, one of the largest cryptocurrency exchanges in the world. It is designed to provide users with a range of benefits, including reduced trading fees, access to exclusive trading tools, and other perks. The LEO Token is based on the Ethereum blockchain and is ERC20 compliant.

For more information about LEO Token, visit the official website at https://www.bitfinex.com/leo.
